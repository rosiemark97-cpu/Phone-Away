How it works

Simple for staff. Seamless for pupils.

The pouch system adds less than two minutes to your school day — for the morning collection and the end-of-day unlock combined. No IT infrastructure. No installation. No wall damage.

1

Phone in, pouch sealed

Pupils arrive and place their phone in their personal lockable pouch. A member of staff holds the unlock magnet to seal it. One or two seconds per pupil. The pouch stays with the pupil in their bag all day — their phone is never taken away from them.

2

Locked all day, no friction

The sealed pouch cannot be opened without the handheld magnet. No apps. No passwords. No wall stations. Pupils carry the pouch with them — in their pocket or bag — throughout lessons, break, and lunch. Supply staff need no training beyond a laminated card.

3

Unlocked at the gate

At the end of the day, one member of staff stands at the exit with the handheld magnet. A single sweep unlocks each pouch in under two seconds. One person can unlock 300 pupils before they reach the pavement. No queues. No stations. No installation.

💡
The magnet ratio that saves you money: Unlike competitors who suggest one unlocking station per 100 pupils, a single handheld magnet covers your entire school. Most schools need just 2–4 magnets — one per keyholder. A 1,200-pupil school needs the same number of magnets as a 300-pupil school. This changes the economics of the system entirely.
